Salmon Fishing on the Duwamish River

In this website is will go over the best methods to catch fish in The Green/Duwamish river systems. I will also go over basic rules and regulations that everyone should know.

click

Thats me with one of my first coho salmon, that I caught on the Duwamish this year. Using these methods you can catch some too!

Fishing with salmon eggs

Fishing with salmon eggs is the most used method on the Green and Duwamish river systems. You can use eggs by floeating them on a bobber, or drifting them with a light weight and a 4-6 foot leader. Fishing with eggs works best during low tide.

Fishing with spinners

Spinners are also very effective on this river. i recommend using blue fox #4, mepps flying c, and mepps aglia spinners. I recomend flashy and lighter colors like pink, red and orange; but they all work great. these work the best in calm areas of the river where fish are holding, and not during low tide.

Regulations

Regulations change every year. Before fishing you need to check the regulations on the specific part of the river that you are going to fish at, because fish limits and regs change. Make sure to always fill out your catch card when you keep a fish. To find Regulations you can download the fish washington app, or go to https://wdfw.wa.gov/fishing
